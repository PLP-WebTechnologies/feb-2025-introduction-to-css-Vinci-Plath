About ClimaHub

ClimaHub is a platform dedicated to amplifying the voices of women and girls affected by climate disasters. We provide:

  • Storytelling spaces for climate survivors
  • Mental health and emotional support resources
  • Access to financial aid opportunities
  • Advocacy tools to drive policy change
  • A safe and empowering community network
